### Clean Skills
- **Phase-Ordered Plan**: Always execute clean tasks in the fixed sequence: 1) locate & acquire target object, 2) bring it to an available water source/sink to clean, 3) navigate to final location, 4) place object.
  _Apply when: As soon as the goal specifies the object must be clean before placement._
- **Pick Before You Wander**: If the target object is visible or discovered, take it immediately; never leave it behind to explore other places, as possession enables direct cleaning and prevents redundant searches.
  _Apply when: After visually confirming the presence of the target object on any surface or inside an opened container._
- **Sink First for Cleaning**: Upon holding the target object, go straight to the nearest sink, basin, or faucet and issue the clean/use command before any placement attempts.
  _Apply when: Once the target object is in hand and its required state is ‘clean’._
- **Systematic Container Sweep**: When the object isn’t found on open surfaces, iterate through all unopened or unexamined containers in the room before revisiting already-checked spots to avoid search loops.
  _Apply when: After initial obvious surfaces are empty and the target object remains unfound._
- **State Verification Before Drop**: Always inspect or infer the object’s state after cleaning; if still not confirmed clean, re-clean before final placement to satisfy goal conditions.
  _Apply when: Immediately after a cleaning action and before placing the object at its target location._
- **Use Location Priors**: Begin search at the most probable category-specific surfaces (e.g., kitchenware on countertop/stove, food on dining table) to minimize exploration steps and avoid aimless roaming.
  _Apply when: At the very start of the task to choose the first search destination for the target object._
