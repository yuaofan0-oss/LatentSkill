### Pick And Place Skills
- **Systematic First-Pass Search**: Maintain a checklist of all visible and closed containers/surfaces; open or inspect each unseen candidate exactly once before revisiting any location.
  _Apply when: After reading the goal and before acquiring every required object._
- **Grab When Seen**: Whenever a needed object is visible and reachable, immediately take it before moving elsewhere—do not leave targets uncollected.
  _Apply when: Upon first sight of an unheld object that matches the goal specification._
- **Transform Before Transport**: If the goal specifies a state change (e.g., heated, cooled, cleaned), perform the transformation at the nearest appropriate appliance before heading to the final destination.
  _Apply when: Right after acquiring an object that must change state._
- **Place Before More Search**: When holding any goal object and the target location is known and reachable, navigate there and place it immediately, then resume searching if more items are needed.
  _Apply when: While carrying a required object and the destination has been identified._
- **Track Counts for Multiples**: Maintain a tally of how many target objects are required, held, and already placed; stop searching only after the placed count meets the goal.
  _Apply when: Throughout tasks demanding two or more instances of the same object._
